About us

History behind Festißal

It all started in April 2011 when Ângelo, Rogério, César, Daniel and João longtime friends, decided to organize a group trip to the world famous FestiBal. When organizing the festival, they had many doubts: "should we camp or stay in a hotel?", "is this hostel good? Does it give us the best quality/price?", "is it better to buy the VIP ticket or the general admission ticket ?" and "Should we book local transport?". The planning and booking process was very complicated, time consuming and very expensive!

History behind Festißal

That year, they ended up not going to Boom Festival. Instead, they embarked on a mission: to bring the festival and music community together on a central booking platform to help make these processes more open, aware and connected to real needs. After a few weeks of brainstorming (and many Skype sessions), Festißal was born.
