Privacy Policy

1. Who is the data controller?

The data controller is the Japan company FestiBal SOUND, with corporate address at C/ Roc Boronat, 142 3º planta 08018 Barcelona (Spain) (“FestiBal”).

2. Who is the data protection officer?

The data protection officer acts as a liaison between FestiBal and you, to ensure FestiBal’s compliance with the data protection legislation and guarantee your rights under such legislation. You can contact the data protection officer of FestiBal at privacy@festibal.com.

3. For which purposes does FestiBal process your personal data?

Concerning the FestiBal Festival organized by FestiBal, S.L. in the city of Barcelona, FestiBal processes your personal data for the following purposes:

a. When you have purchased tickets through our website, to (i) process your order, (ii) receive and process your payment, (iii) provide customer support in relation to the tickets purchased and (iv) any other activity related to the selling of tickets.

b. Send you commercial communications, when you have given your consent, which will contain information about the FestiBal festival held in Barcelona or any other upcoming events, products or services related to the FestiBal brand to provide you with a better experience as well as other FestiBal festivals that take place in other cities.

c. Send you customer support e-mails, which may include, among others, reservation confirmations, event reminders or event modifications and information concerning the access to the venue.

d. Guarantee the security and safety of the festival premises, assets and individuals and, if applicable, to investigate any possible incidents that may take place during the festival. This may include video surveillance systems.

    As per the FestiBal festivals organized by third parties and held outside the city of Barcelona, FestiBal will only process your personal data to:

    e. Be able to globally manage all the FestiBal festivals that take place in different cities, to evaluate the performance of those festivals and identify which are the modifications that need to be introduced in order to offer a better experience to the festival-goers.

    f. When you have given your consent, send you commercial communications, which will contain information about a specific FestiBal festival or any other upcoming events, products or services related to the FestiBal brand to provide you with a better experience, as well as other FestiBal festivals that take place in other cities.

      When accessing to FestiBal website and mobile application, FestiBal processes your personal data for the following purposes:

      g. Whenever you sign up for a FestiBal account via our website www.festibal.com or in one of our apps, to manage your account or any of the applications available in the referred website (for instance, the “FestiBal Forum” section).

      h. To respond to your requests of information and/or suggestions made through the “Contact Form” available at the FestiBal website.

      i. From social media platforms such as Spotify, Google and Facebook who collect your personal data in their capacity of data controller and obtain your consent (where legally required) to share such data with us, in order to enable FestiBal to obtain information about your media consumption and your artists preferences when using the referred social platforms.

      j. Send you commercial communications, when you have given your consent, which will contain information about the FestiBal festival held in Barcelona or any other upcoming events, products or services related to the FestiBal brand to provide you with a better experience, as well as about other FestiBal festivals that take place in other cities.

        4. Which are the lawful bases to process your personal data?

        Processing activities indicated under Section 3 (a) and (g): the fulfillment of our contract with you (and, in particular, our Terms and Conditions) when purchasing the tickets to attend the FestiBal Festival or when you create an account in our website.

        Processing activities indicated under Section (b), (f), (h), (i) and (j): the consent given by you when checking the checkbox available on the website, to receive commercial communications, to respond to your request for information and/or suggestions and to obtain information about your media consumption and artist preferences.

        Processing activities under Section 3 (c), (d), and (e): the legitimate interest of FestiBal in processing your personal data for the purposes indicated in the referred sections. In this respect, we consider that the mentioned legitimate interest, on which FestiBal bases the processing of your personal data, does respect your fundamental rights and freedoms since your personal data is being processed to improve as a company and offer attendees to the FestiBal a better experience at their festivals. In any case, you may exercise your right to object by addressing such request to privacy@festibal.com.

        In countries where the legitimate interest is not a legal basis for processing personal data, by accepting this Privacy Policy you will be granting your express consent to all the processing activities listed under Section 3 (c), (d) and (e).

        5. How long FestiBal will retain your personal data?

        FestiBal will process your personal data as long as:

        1. You do not object to the reception of FestiBal commercial communications. If you have objected to the reception of commercial communications, FestiBal will keep the required personal data in order to attend your request to no longer receive said informative alerts;

        2. Is processing a request made by you, through any of the channels indicated under Section 3; and,

        3. The purposes for which the personal data is processed are being achieved.

        In any case, FestiBal may retain your personal data until the end of the statues of limitation of any liabilities that may arise and during the term required to comply with any applicable legal obligation.

        6. With whom does FestiBal share your personal data?

        Your personal data may be shared with:

        1. Providers of products and services hired by FestiBal as data processors, to achieve the mentioned purposes, such as reception and security services of the premises, maintenance services for video surveillance or IT and cloud services.

        2. Local Organizers in charge of the management of each specific festival, as data processors. Your personal data will only be shared with them in the event FestiBal deems it necessary, so the Local Organizers can carry out specific commercial communications. Local Organizers may share your personal data with FestiBal for the purposes described in Section 3 (e) and (f).

        3. Competent authorities as well as other third parties interested in investigating an illegal act and, if applicable, establishing, exercising or defending the actions or claims always fulfilling the applicable legal requirements.

        If your personal data is accessed from countries that do not offer an adequate level of protection, FestiBal will adopt, if required, appropriate safeguards on such international data transfers in accordance with the applicable data protection legislation.

        FestiBal does not share your personal data with any other third parties, unless required by applicable law.

        7. Which are the sources from where we obtain your personal data and which categories of personal data do we process?

        FestiBal only processes personal data that is relevant to the purposes identified in Section 3. In particular, FestiBal may collect your name, last name, phone number, physical address, email address, device identification, IP address and payment details (if applicable). The referred personal data is directly obtained from the data subject.

        8. Which are your rights when you provide us with your personal data?

        Depending on your country of residence, you may have the ability to exercise any of the following rights:

        • Access: you may access your data included in FestiBal files.

        • Rectification/Modification: you may modify your personal data if these are inaccurate.

        • Erasure: you may request that your personal data are erased.

        • Objection: you may request that your personal data are not processed.

        • Portability: you may receive, in an electronic file, the personal data that you provided FestiBal with, as well as send it to different entities.

        • Restriction of processing: you may request a restriction on how your data is processed when: (i) the accuracy of your personal data is being verified after you have contested its accuracy, (ii) the processing of your data is illegal and you oppose to the erasure of your data; (iii) FestiBal does no longer need the personal data for the purposes of the processing, but they are required by you for the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims, (iv) you have objected to the processing for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or necessary for the purposes of a legitimate interest, whilst it is being verified whether the legitimate grounds of FestiBal override yours.

        • Opt-Out: you can request to stop receiving our promotional emails by following the instructions in any marketing email you get from us. You can also change your preferences in your account. It may take some days to process your request. Even if you opt out of getting marketing emails, we will still be sure to send you transactional messages.

        Data subjects located in Brazil also have the following data subject rights:

        • Confirmation: you may ask for confirmation of the existence of the processing.

        • Information about the possibility of denying consent and the consequences of such denial.

        • Right to petition concerning your personal data, against FestiBal before the national authority.

        • Right to request a review of decisions made solely on the basis of automated processing, when applicable.

        • Information about public and private entities with whom we share your data, when applicable.

        • Concerning the right of objection indicated above, to request that your personal data are not processed when based on one of the situation of waiver consent, if there is non-compliance with the provisions of the applicable law.

        • Request the anonymization, blocking or erasure of your personal data, insofar as it is considered unnecessary, excessive or non-compliant.

        Additionally, at any time, you may withdraw your consent without affecting the data processing carried out before such withdrawal. In order to exercise your rights and withdraw your consent, you can send your request to privacy@festibal.com, together with a copy of your ID or passport or any other valid document evidencing your identity.

        In any case, you also have the right to lodge a complaint before the Spanish Data Protection Authority (www.aepd.es) or the data protection authority in your country of residence.

We will also give you a list of the third parties that have received your information. You can make a request one time each year.

        • Disclosure. You have the right to request a report showing the personal information collected, shared, and sold about you in the past 12 months.

        • Opt-Out. You have the right to opt-out to the sale of your personal information to third parties.

        • Deletion. You have the right to request that we delete any personal information collected from you.

        • Non-Discrimination. We shall not discriminate against you based on your exercise of any of the above rights.

        You may submit a request to enact any of the above rights by sending an email to privacy@festibal.com. Please be sure to include your full name and which type of request you are making in your email.

        To verify your identity, we may collect information such as your full name and email address. This information will be compared to our existing records to verify the identity of the individual submitting the request. We may also require you to confirm your request via an automated email message before responding to your CCPA rights request. Depending on the sensitivity of the information requested and the type of request, we may require additional information to verify your identity before responding. We will respond to your request within 45 days if possible and required under the law.
